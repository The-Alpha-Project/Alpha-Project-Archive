Blizzard I liked all your games. Blizzard is for me an indicator of good, challenging and highly enjoyable games. I must admit the idea with instanced PvP is very good, very new for MMORPG and will certainly be enjoyed by many. By many, but not by all.

And that is the point. When I first read that Blizzard will make a MMORPG I was very excited. A MMORPG with the usual Blizzard guarantee in a high fun factor. But for me a free PvP adds alot of fun.

(may free PvP be Team PvP, or allvsall, or even lvl restricted with a GOOD range up and down)

1. Free PvP adds (mmo)ROLEPLAYING(game)
(Look and read the story
http://www.battle.net/forums/wow/thread.aspx?FN=wow-general&T=1127309&P=1&ReplyCount=77#post1127309)

2. Free PvP adds a more action as well as atmosphere to the Game

When I walk around with free PvP I always have in Mind: "Was that and Orc behind those rocks? What was this shadow? A Wolf? Or a druid? I better go off and get some friends here!"
When I walk into a PvP gate and be portaled to the instanced PvP zone im completly aware that there will soon be an enemy that strikes me, there is almost NO atmosphere.

I think this atmosphere adds alot to an mmoRPG. Everyone pls remember her or his first days in her or his first MMORPG. Werent they dark, and new and mysterious? They were like that, because you didnt know whats behind the next rock!

What makes a Horror film so frightening? What makes people scared? The atmosphere of not knowing what comes next, the moment when the evil comes around the corner. But when I know, in this zone the worse that can happen to me is a snake or a badger .....

3. Dont let WoW become Counterstrike.... or Warcraft 3....
The instanced PvP reminds me much on "connecting to server, or pushing quickplay button"

Please Blizzard, make a MassivlyMultiplayerOnlineROLEPLAYINGGAME

Please make a good one... and pls... add PvP servers with some really cool Blizzard rules. Pls add ROLEPLAYING :-)
