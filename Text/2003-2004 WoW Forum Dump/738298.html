Now we continue to the criticism:

Paint Shop can trick anyone into thinking a drawing is good.

The tiger needs face work, it appears you're not good with full animal anatomy.

The night elf's face is the only decent thing about her;

  • she is unproportioned
  • has the stomach of a 50 year old woman
  • her legs are unproportioned
  • you need to work on shoulders
  • the hair going over one of her breasts looks odd in that it makes that breast look smaller than the other.

  • arms are good.

  • The spirit wolf looks almost professional, but sometimes we make lucky drawing mistakes that trick the eye and allow images to appear above our skill level.
  • You did so well on the face with the nelf, where did it go here?
  • muscle anatomy could use some work
  • The CG is prohibiting full criticism.


Once again this is all constructive and I am no way bashing you. Criticism motivates perfection. Other than that, they are pretty good drawings.
