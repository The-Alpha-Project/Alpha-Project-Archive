8. Re: Could trolls be the bastard NE race? | 12/3/2003 2:31:32 AM GMTST
There is such a thing in evolution as analogous structures. This means structures that aren't really the same, but accomplish the same effect. This is a result of selection: basically, the environment favors a particular set of traits.

Yes, the rules of evolution can apply in a magical world. In fact, they're more interesting in such an environment, because if magic can induce mutations, then it's even more likely that evolution takes place at an accelerated rate in such an environs. Which would account for the rapid changes of the Naga in a meager 10,000 years... and the possibility for entirely different species, with creator entities...

But, back to the original argument. Two species can grow to be similar because the same things will give them the advantage in the same environment. This would account for the structural similarity of the Elves and the Trolls. No shared ancestry is needed that close on the evolutionary chain... Trolls are not necessarily sylvan sapiens horribus next to the Night Elves' sylvan sapiens noctem.

I mean, both trolls and elves are sentient, and thus far-ranging, but they did 'evolve', if you would, in the same general environment. The ancestral trolls might have looked somewhat different, same with the ancestral elves, but being that both have a tendency towards forest environments, they grew to adapt, including such traits as big ears for hearing, etc.
