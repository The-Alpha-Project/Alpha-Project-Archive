135. Re: Funniest game moments | 1/25/2004 1:05:17 PM GMTST
I was playing BF1942 just a few hours ago.

We're playing Midway, and its a 3v3, we're all having fun. I'm a Scout, and I'm doing Scouty things, including capping any Axis who sticks his head above the horizon.

Finally, it appears I got the other team angry. I'm the kind of guy that gets really into it when I snipe, and this one guy was dodging in and out of their building on top of a hill. From my vantage point laying down, the only thing he could see was the barrel of my gun, and as soon as he THOUGHT about leaving that building, I'd take a shot. He kept either avoiding them or taking non-fatal shots and healing inside, and I was getting really focused on taking him out. He came out of the building prone, and as soon as he moved just a little I put a bullet in his head.

Now, we're playing this in a PC cafe a block down from my house (incredibly huge monitors, and the owner is the uncle of my girlfriend, so we don't get charged jack as long as the place isn't crowded with paying customers). Once I shoot the guy, I yell out "GOTCHA!" I stand up, turn around, and there is this giant tank literally looking right at me. The cannon fires, I'm blown like 20 feet into the sky before my body falls down into the valley. And my friend says "sniping is easier with a cannon."

Like twenty minutes later, we were getting pounded. All we had was our base at the far north of the island, and our ships. They were using planes and tanks (1 plane, 2 tanks) and were just ripping us apart. I was on AA, and shot down the plane, and immediately ran into the water.

My friend sees this, and yells out "the bastard is swimming! Strafe him!" The dead flier resurrects and gets into a plane, and keeps trying to turn me into fish food. I finally make it onto the battleship, and use the machine gun on him, knocking him out of the sky. Immediately, I fire a volley of turret fire at the coast randomly, then to the sub (using the boat to move faster), and submerge.

Thinking I'm still on the battleship, he flies back and keeps trying to bomb the ship. Meanwhile, I come up by their ships and sink all 3 of 'em. As he flies back, I submerge and go back to my battleship, and knock him out of the sky AGAIN.

I turn the turrets back to shore, looking at my ally's monitor next to me to gauge where my shells are landing. I shoot one that hits behind and to the right of our base, so I fix the range as this giant battle is going on. I fire...

And knock out BOTH of my allies. Simultaneously. With a direct hit of the turrets. They go FLYING, almost as high as the plane; I highly doubt tanks are designed to be airborne.

Everyone is now just laughing as hard as they can, and one of my friends on the other team says "thanks!" and walks into the base to take it. Stupid, since he's soon a shell-sized crater, too. The flier friend comes back and bombs the battleship, but it doesn't sink.

Now, I swear on this part.

I'm pretty sure we're losing, so I decide to go out in glory. I raise my cannons as high as they go, and turn towards the length of my ship. The flier has turned around for another pass, and as he flies over the battleship length-wise, I blast his out of the air. With the ships main turrets. Everyone sees this (they were watching the monitors, again, almost TV sized) and it was just absolutely hilarious.

And the cherry on top of the sundae? The remaining shells land directly on my battleship, sinking it.

I sunk my battleship!
