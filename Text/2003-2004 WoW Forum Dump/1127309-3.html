41. Re: The perfect WoW PvP-wise (long) | 2/2/2004 10:14:25 PM GMTST
How bout this idea? Scaling damage/damage reduce based on level difference. These are just numbers I'm estimating, but you'll get the point.

Let's say a lvl 60 is feeling mean and wants to take out a lvl 5 in full FvF PvP. Normally, the lvl 5 is basically dead. He/she may be able to call out some higher lvl faction member, but their own fate is still sealed. However, this idea of scaling damage/damage reduce may help.

For every lvl higher that the higher (griefer) character is than the lower one, the lower character will get a total damage boost of 10%. In this case, it's 55 x 5 = 275% total damage boost to the lower character. Also, the higher lvl character will have THEIR total damage reduced by 1% for every lvl they are higher than the lower character, so in this case, it's 60 - 5 = 55% taken off, which means they're only doing 45% of their original damage.

With less damage being done to the griefed, and they doing more damage, it may very well help the griefed stand a much better chance. However, I would still it would seem very off for a lvl 60 to die to a lvl 5, so I would still be expecting the lvl 5 to die. However, the lvl 5 would prove to be a much harder fight. A small band of lvl 5's may be even enough to take out a lvl 60, which would help stop griefing just for the sake of it. I mean, what lvl 60 would want to admit they were taken out by some lvl 5's?

This would also promote group work should there be any high lvls near by. So, you may have to group for a little while if you are low lvl, but you still have the freedom to solo later on. What about two fighting characters close in lvl? A 10% damage boost and a 1% damage reduce will not matter very much then, so against similar lvl characters, it is nearly even. Keep in mind the higher character is still higher.

This would help stop stupid griefing, and at the very same time keep a realistic FvF PvP. This system (though the numbers could use some tweaking I suppose) seems like the best option to me.
