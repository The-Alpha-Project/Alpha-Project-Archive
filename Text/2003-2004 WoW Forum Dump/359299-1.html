16. Re: Semi OT: War3x Music | 8/1/2003 4:56:42 PM GMTDT
1.) Open the Frozen Throne World Editor.
2.) Open the Sound Editor. (Button at the top, looks like the standard Windows speaker thing, next to the helmet)
3.) Sounds < Internal < Sound < Music < mp3Music < PH1.mp3
4.) Highlight it on the list and click File < Export Sound...
5.) Pick a name and folder, and it's there.
