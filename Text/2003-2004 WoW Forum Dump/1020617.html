Post stories of funniest times you ever had while playing a game, online or otherwise:

ill start off with a couple.

For any of you that have ever played Diablo 2, you will enjoy these:

For a while, there was a weird glitch where if you equipped items after having died, before you retrieved your body, and then died again before retrieving your body, all your original equipment would "pop out" of your corpse. So I was PvPing this guy with some friends, and he bragged endlessly about his uber equipment. And the guy did have it all...all the best items at that time. he was decked out. we managed to kill him, and somehow, he must have accidently picked up some old regular armor on the ground that an enemy left when he was trying to get to his body. Being naked, and doing what he would always do to us, we killed him naked. Immediately, his body popped and all his great equipment lay on the ground. So of course we picked it ALL up and had a great time chatting in game about who wanted what, while he endlessly cursed at us, and then begged us to give it all back. He threatened us with his clan's retribution and we just laughed at him all the time. Ive never laughed so hard as when i saw his stuff pop.


Another one that used to bring me joy.. There was this boss called Hephasto, that you could go to right before you got to Diablo's Sanctuary. You didnt HAVE to fight him, but you could. I always loved to get on Hell difficulty, wait until people were fighting Diablo (he took a while to beat for a couple of patches) and go find Hephasto. Heph was really fast, and could keep up with a running character. I would have him follow me all the way to where the players were fighting Diablo and watch the mayhem as he slew every player.
