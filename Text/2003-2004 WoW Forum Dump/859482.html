236. Re: What I learned at Gen Con today. | 12/15/2003 8:54:57 PM GMTST
I ll just post my stuff here.. i think a lot of peeps read it already cause it shows on the count.. but as this one is sticky :

First, lemme say that I'm not a a enlgish speaker, so this won't be always easy to understand :)

I spent 6 hours there with the blizzar team, they were all very nice and replied all my question..
A lot of what I found is in other report so i won't repeat.. I'm not sure this is in other report so sorry if it's repeating.

In general i was suprised how good looking it is.. In pics I was always 'yeah it s ok not super good looking but ok'.. When u see it moving you are just wow.. it looks GREAT!

Voice:
Players will have a voice for some action then do.. I heard a Troll saying 'I have no target' When the plaer was trying to cast a lighitng without a target.Or 'I am out of mana'.. This only the player will hear it..
But you have voice emote for your teammate for this you have to type '/v emote' like '/v inc' and your toon say in group 'incoming!'.. I saw about 10 voice emote.

Player housing, is not in it yet but they are thinking about it, they are not sure it will be in for release. Same thing for player shop like the 'bazzar' in EQ.

Mount: They couldn't show mount cause the highest level they could make was 25 and for now the minimum requirement is 30.

Boat and Zeplin are not in the game yet.

High end mobs: They couldn't show us cause it was too hard to access but they told me there would be a lot of high mob, like dragons, demons and a lot of stuff..

Underwater: was really nice, there are huge numbrer of details.. i was looking and couldn t stop saying 'damn that s nice.. damn that s nice..'

Pets:
Hunter: Everyone knows the pet has is own xp pool to lvl up. But the skills they will get is not random anymore, you have to find a <xanimal Trainer> in the world.. Like a tiger will need a 'Cat Trainer' and the player CHOOSE the skills his animal wants.. Whcih is way better IMO. They are still not sure if u can only have one pet.

Warlock: To get a pet u need to do a quest, When this quest is done, you do a duel with the pet you want, if you win, this pet will be yours and u can bring him up when u want. Pets have different use: Voidwalker will be the tank, imp the caster and some ohter will be damage dealer and other will be buffer.
When you cast a pet, a second hotkey bar will pop showing all your pet abilities.
The warlock pet lvl up with the Warlock, it doesn't have his own xp. (would be too hard to lvl up 4 differnts pets..)

You can rename your pet when u want.You get pets pretty early.. like level 4 - 5.

Money:
Gold, will be take time to get gold.. they don't want people to sell a sword for 1000000 gold.. So they say it will be hard to get already 1gp..

Hero is not impletement yet but they might have several Hero u can choose depending of you class.. and this would depends of your skills.. I think When u are a hero u get new skills.. but i m not sure i understood everything there.

Tavern: there will be game or funny stuff.. you can t be drunk yet but they are thinking about it.. they said that you could go to a tavern and see fights and funny stuffs.

Races: they were all looking good imo.. except the troll female that was kinda weird..
I know that Gnome will have advantage at tinkering. And Dorf start with a 'find treasure' abilities..
I think they have advantage at finds herb.. but i'm not sure

Class: every class have his 'mana kind' pool, caster will have mana, Warrior/ Druid form/rogue will have Rage (raise when u do damage/take damage), hunter focus (raise if u stay still).

There are some skills that some race/class are not supposed to get, but if you work hard on it (thru quest) you MIGHT be able to use them.. They gave me exemple but i forgot.

Aggro management: Mobs will aggro Depending on you level and his lvl.. If you are way higher then him you will need to be closer to aggro, if you are lower it will aggro from far away..

I talked to them about mage stuff, and they just told me their spell were awsome. every spells have it s own animation.. when he cast a fire wall you see the mage puttin his hand behind (like a kameha from Dragon ball hehe) and u see a ball of fire forming.. getting bigger.. then it go to his target, zizzagging.. looking really great

There are box you can open, Rogue can picklock (doesn t generate any aggro) but mage can open it with a spell to blast it.. but with a blast you can get aggro from mob around cause they hear it..
Once open you can loot it like a corpse..

It s really nice IMO.
I think I can be trapped too.


Emote:
All the emote are differnt for the races.. Gnome dance is really funny.. it s like spanking around and dancing.. The emote dancing doesn t stop untill you move.. u can combo theemote.. If you /dance and in the middle you do /wave, it stop dancing to wave then restart dancing.
Undead dancing like if he was listening a rock stuff, hand in the hair and moving is head up and down.. nice looking ;)

XP:
With you lvl X, all mob u kill of lvl Y will worth the same xp..
Except in dungeon, when u see a mob with + near his lvl it means it has AT LEAST 3X more HP, like this fight will last longer, and u can use ur discipline /rage, ect..

You get more XP like that too..


Items:
They didn t put in it the ubber item but they are working on it.. the dev i talked to told me that was the next step..
A lot of items are rare drop from mob, and you can see someone has it cause it show on your toon.

Equipement, you can see everypiece of item you put on u, if you change ur belt, u see it in your toon.. very neat..
For guild, there is a graphic you can see on your toon.

The head are moving, following the person you are lookint at.. And the eyes are blinking.

Duel is the only PVP in it yet, they are not sure if PVP in arena should be everyone against everyone or it should go like duel.. u have to both agree
(i'd rather see it like anarchy, everyone hit who they wants)

I spent like 1 full hour with the guy at the end and he showed me EVERYTHING i wanted.. I was damn lucky..
At the end i didn't want leave .. came home and saw EQ.. i was so ready to trade my very hihg lvl/great geared toon for a place in the beta..



There are other stuff but i forgot sorry ;)
I'll add stuff when i ll remember and if u guys like this.

This game looks so great.. I just can't wait..
