33. Re: An Actual Idea | 1/22/2004 4:44:23 PM GMTST
Hrmmm....very interesting.

Yeah, I was waiting for someone to spring to whole "different billing periods" thing on me... I honestly don't have an answer for that, but give a day or two and I'll get back to it.

And yeah, if you read my original post, I do not actually say that it should be a PvP battle, although that was my original thought, because I realize that something like that would seriously hinder the casual gamer. Somewhere along the line it was suggested that there should be a contest reflecting the same difficulty level for any level character. For example, there could be a series of battles against monsters matched up to the level of the participant, with each consecutive battle being harder than the one preceding it. But we couldn't just have this going on forever, so perhaps after some number of these battles if there is more than one player remaining then the contest can become PvP, as monsters are no longer a threat. Maybe this is rewarding the avid gamer a bit, but hey, those guys spend most of their lives doing this kind of thing, you have to acknowledge them somewhere.

Also, I wasn't saying that the player with the most time in game or best equipment was the best player, but that he or she was at least one of the most experienced players, so if that player and another player with less experience were at the same level and fighting the same monster, the experienced player would know what to do to defeat the monster due to his/her time in game. I agree, level and equipment mean nothing as far as skill and knowledge are concerned.
