1. Dynamic PvP zones | 6/24/2003 3:57:08 AM GMTDT
This is something I am really hoping will make it into WoW.
The way I see it is, since the game is centered around the horde-alliance conflict, have some of the pvp zones be your basic arena thingies, and others be massive battles.
Example: Blizzard makes a newspost that a Horde invasion force has landed on the shores of Lordaeron, and a PvP zone opens up there. The zone would stay there for about a week, and depending on which side "won" the battle, either a new zone would open up further in lordaeron, or perhaps the alliance forces retaliate from kalimdor into Horde territory, opening a pvp zone there.
These battles would of course never go into the towns themselves, or anywhere near the lvl 1 starting areas, as those are the capitals of the various races anyway.

I've thought about something like this, but I think it is very very hard to implement.

I think that either the in game GMs or perhaps the top ranked guilds or players should be able to choose, weekly (or variable), a target area/zone to siege or capture.

Within that week that area becomes a warzone for the two factions. Of course, there would have to be a day warning before this begins so that newbie characters would have a chance to haul their butts out of the oncoming danger if they choose.

"You hear the sounds of war afoot, make haste from this area or risk peril!" could pop-up on your screen when you zone or log into the area.
