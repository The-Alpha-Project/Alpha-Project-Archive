I am not fond of the idea of once per server items. Doesn't leave alot to look forward to if you missed out on getting it.

What would be cool, and would be available to basiclly everyone who put the time into it would be truly unique, personalized items.

Basiclly after a long quest, which I won't detail since that is kinda irrelevant, suffice it to say it should be long and challenging, you get the opportunity to make your item.

You'd first pick the type of weapon. This could be done via dialog to keep it in character.

Then pick the model for your weapon. To keep it in context with the game world, perhaps done by choosing the mold(s) for the weapon.

Then the colour the weapon will be. Enamels, blood of exotic creatures, stains, etc.

Then the colour of the weapons particle effect as well as type. Or for in-character terms, the enchantment placed on it.

Then finally you get to name the weapon. I'd suggest a character limit, but allow spaces (but no special characters). Name would have to pass filters to make sure it wasn't already in use or considered obscene/offensive.

Stats and bonuses I think would be based on your class.

That's the sort of unique items I think would be awesome for the game. And they would truly be unique.
