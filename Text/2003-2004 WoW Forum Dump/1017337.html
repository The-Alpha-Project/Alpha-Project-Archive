141. Re: A Role-Player's Plea [Or Several] | 1/19/2004 9:10:41 AM GMTST
So, you're saying that the PC that strikes the killing blow on the Lich King should be converted into a GM-controlled replacement of the Lich King until they are slain in turn? It's an interesting concept idea, but I don't think it would turn out well for several reasons.

The player may not wish to be subjected to that kind of thing. It's very difficult to say if a player possesses the rights to their character in a MMORPG completely, but I suspect this kind of thing could be the grounds for a law suit if it isn't consentual.

Second, it doesn't work in a role-playing sense, unless the player is willingly sacrificing themself to merge with the Lich King. I suspect this would not be the intent of most players who are trying to kill the Lich King/Arthas.

It would be somewhat more concievable that a player would suffer ill effects if they take the Crown of the Lich King or Frostmourne. I do not believe converting them into a GM NPC is a viable option, however, unless the player specifically requested something along those lines and Blizzard decided to comply for some reason.

142. Re: A Role-Player's Plea [Or Several] | 1/19/2004 6:26:06 PM GMTST
Technically speaking, as soon as you put an original work in any form of media (names included), its considered copywritten by United States Federal Law. This includes MMORPG's, or any other game. You "may" be able to skirt around it with the EULA, but even that can be argued in court, and when you have law dealing across states, that makes it more difficult, especially when there's federal laws looming overhead. Odds are, Blizzard would lose that battle, unless they got express permission from the player to use their name.

I think Blizzard may be off to a good start with alot of the functions that they are going to be promoting in WoW, to help promote the game as a role playing game, and not a l33t loot fest, that so many other games turn out to be.

If they can have GM events, and help get people involved more, I think with their already established foundation that the game can be something truly spectacular.
