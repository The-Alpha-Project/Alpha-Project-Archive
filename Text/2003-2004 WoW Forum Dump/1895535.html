MMORPGS are just an evolution from the first games ever such as Final Fantasy and Dragon Warrior on Nintendo. Just evolves with technology ... graphics, sound, processing power, and internet related technologies.

Blizzard is a typical successful company because they understand how to sell a product. First you take a product that is very popular and high in demand. Then you investigate that product, who make the best and why is it the best. Also they investigate all the things that are good and bad about the product.
Now they create their own product with all good things, and fix all the bad things about it. And then they give their own creative unique feel to it. And thats how business is done. Just like software, or cell phone design and assembly, or even food products like doritoes and canned soda.

The reason WoW will do good, is because Blizzard has proven on many games that they put out some of the best games ever. It's not just luck that millions of people buy and play Blizzard games for years.

Sure Blizzard is a noob in MMORPG. But they hired people who know how to program it, and they invite power gamer freakazoids to give their input on whats good and whatnot. Blizzard has brains and creativity, they cannot fail.

On the other had, Verant/SOE got lucky with EverQuest. It was the first of its kind (3-D MMORP), with a real sense of fantasy. So players flocked to it and loved it... for a while. EverQuest was extremly addictive to most people for many reasons.
Then after seeing so many other MMORPGS come and go, you realize that SOE just got lucky to be the first. I think SOE sucks. Verant was good, but SOE screwed over EQ.


I cannot wait until WoW comes out. IMO the graphics are way better than EQ2. I have seen all from both games( Screenshots, and movie downloads).
WOW is more colorful and cartoonish. Like an explosion of watercolor and a perfect composition of american and anime animation.
All the music I have heard from WOW is excellent, unlike EQ where it is just a jumble of 20 midi tracks overlapping.

TOo many things I like about MMORPGS to list.

So I list the things I don't like:


#1: Not being able to do anything witout a cleric or atleast 2 priest classes.
#2: Not being able to solo unless you are a solo class.
#3: Useless equipment for the risk vs. reward. (EG: level 50 item that has AC and stats of a level 20 item and a completly worthless spell effect)
#4: Useless quests(rewards for quest)
#5: Experience penalties. Most high level raids you will die about 10 times and get 90% rezzed, and thats about equal to 1 completly unrezzed death which is about 4 hours of experience grinding.
#6: NERFING GOOD THINGS THAT WERE VERY VERY VERY HARD TO GET. If somthing is hard to get, don't nerf it. Examine it before you make the item available. (Ex: Donals Breastplate of mourning from Trakanon in EQ, and Dire charm for Enchanters in EQ)
#7: Tradeskills that don't give skill ups and require hours of hunting just to get components for 20 combine attempts. And are very expensive to make, and don't sell for even a fraction.
