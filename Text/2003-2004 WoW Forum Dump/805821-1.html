Ways to avoid powerleveling?

-- Make crafting really fun.

-- Make quests and tasks enjoyable and maybe not so repeatable.

-- Make PvsP, 1on1 dueling and raiding a gas. At least as fun and fast paced as FP-Shooters.

In short make a game that is far different from the online treadmill that plague the genre.
