Hail all!

First of all I have to let u know that I am a so called PKer myself and want to try to explain our view of things without the usual flaming of other types of players.

I think I am talking for alot of the kind as me if I say that we do absolutely NOT want to be on the same server as the "normal" players. "Why?" You might ask, it's simple, we want to be able to attack and be attacked anywhere, anyhow and at anytime... this adds the challenge and thrill we are looking for. We want to be afraid of death, knowing that we can get beaten and loose valuable items. And if someone all sudden can level up as a Non-PK and then decide to go PK at later levels it's cheating in our eyes.

The reason for 100% Full PvP for us is that the game itself isn't that huge challenge after awhile, anyone can beat AI's and reach max level. What makes a good MMORPG with PvP in it fun for us is the challenge from having a hard time to level, having Guild Wars and try to chase other Guilds away from our hunting grounds and so on. We want NO safe zones, and we are prepared to die alot, or else we wouldn't be there.

This Arenas or PvP Zones that is goin to be implemented is not enough for us, there is no point in fighting in those for anything else then perhaps getting a reputation as a skilled PKer. We want more, we want to be able to control areas and resources, to prevent other from playing at "your grounds" and so on, it gives u something more then usual PVE that gets boring after awhile.

I have also heard about someone saying that they don't want the community splitted, well it will awlays be splitted into servers, and that's good. The PKers don't want to hear the "carebeartalk" about Quests etc, and I don't think that the "Carebears" want to hear about our bragging or flaming from PK Battles all the time.

A separate server dedicated for Full 100% PvP is good for all IMHO. And as someone said in this thread, it will bring alot of the Griefers and "trash" with it. We that do PK at full time is ok with that, since we have the possibility to deal with them on our server. This will hopefully also take away some more of the kind of people that enjoys to destroy for other players by trying to pull monsters on them to get them killed, steal other players kills and so on...

If Blizzard decide to NOT make such a server I have to be sorry and thank for me, since at least I do know that a game without the challenge and thrill I seek, and a feeling of always being able to get killed at anytime, wont last that long for me. After 6 months to perhaps 1 year as max u use to have seen the most in a MMORPG's world. And after that, I will just be to bored to play any further.

And last I want to say that I do respect all of you other players and the way you want to have fun in a game, and I hope u will aswell respect PKers like me for having our little odd way of enjoying a MMORPG.

And please, let us do it our way on our own server where we can be left alone without any whining from both sides.
