Eddings and Brooks are two of my favorite authors, but I've seen them mentioned here a lot so I'll name probably my favorite series.

It is Memory, Sorrow and Thorn by Tad Williams (otherland books suck though). These books REALLY draw you into them and immerse you into this story that has everything you could want in a great series: history, action, romance, heros, awesome villians, magic, humor, and dishes it out while keeping a wonderful story. Definatly a must read for any fantasy fan. Each book is very long, but you really don't notice it. I have to warn that the first book takes about 15 (out of 44) chapters to really get the story moving, but once it does you will not look back until you close the cover on the third book :)

Book titles in order:
The DragonBone Chair
Stone of Fairwell
To Green Angel Tower (part I and II)
