1. Meat shields, Alliance > Horde? | 11/26/2003 5:36:21 PM GMTST
I didn't want to confuse people by titling the subject Tanks cause there are tanks in other WC games.

Now is it just me or is WoW VERY caster oriented? Other MMORPG's have about half their classes based on close ranged melee to deal their damage. From the look of things there are only 2 classes designed to tank monsters, paladin and warrior. Obviously paladin is Alliance only so does that give them more options for tanks? Maybe druids in bear form will tank well but that balances out between the factions. Except a favorite will be NE druid compared to Tauren druid which gives the Alliance even more possible tanks besides simply Warriors.

What about pets? Will Hunter and Warlock pets be sturdy enough to handle tanking? If so then the Horde has more pet class options available, 3 hunters and 2 warlocks. I assume if solo play is possible that pets will be quite sturdy and capable as tanks for groups if they can work solo. Obviously skill allocation into the pets will determine how well they do.

So dispite having the physically largest race as well as a very sturdy race (orcs) the Horde may be more caster oriented than the Alliance.

Alliance classes:
Melee = 4 (Warrior, Paladin, Rogue, Hunter)
Caster = 4 (Priest, Druid, Mage, Warlock)

Horde classes:
Melee = 3 (Warrior, Rogue, Hunter)
Caster = 5 (Priest, Druid, Mage, Warlock, Shaman)

Obviously races play a role in the situation. The Horde has 4 races that range from pretty good to very good as warriors while the Alliance goes from poor to just good. Sorry gnome warriors but if you can beat a similarly equiped Tauren warrior then there is something seriously wrong. Lumping Hunter in with melee is iffy at best. I'm sure 99% will be ranged but they use weapons, not magic to deal their damage.

Anyone else think Horde groups will have more casters than the average Alliance groups? The racial identities of the Alliance tends more towards casters but there is more variety in the Horde. There are lots of paladin lovers and I wouldn't be surprised to see more of them than warriors in the Alliance.
