Here let me explain this slow for you...

The Lich King CREATED the Order of the Death Knights, they are a group and the main generals of the Scourges Military. They have sworn total allegiance to the Lich King and his cause and will not be swayed away from his utter rule, they have sold their souls to him.

When the Lich King accepts a Death Knight into the Order, he had forged a special blade, a Vampiric Runeblade, which was imbued with intelligence and given personally to the Death Knight as a sign of his status among the other Death Knights. These blades are only forged by the powers of the Lich King and only have one master untill the master dies.

If in case the original Death Knight does perish, the Vampiric Runeblade will drop to the ground and stop emanating magical energy, the sword ACTS like it is just another Bastard Sword. Once someone comes across the sword and picks it up, the sword will awaken its consciousness and slowly corrupt the new weilder... The sword will begin to corrupt his mind making him think of nothing but Chaos and Death, while trying to subdue thoughts of Caring and Compassion. Once the host is corrupted enough, the sword compels him to find the other Death Knights, and once he does, he is brought before the Lich King and added as one of the Order, at which point the Blade fully awakens in all its magical glory.

So all in all... to sum it up.

To be a Death Knight you need to be a MEMBER of the Death Knights Order, to do that... you NEED to work for the Lich King and be granted a Runeblade. Even if you find the Runeblade without joining the Order, you WILL be compeled to join anyways. Hope that helped.
