If you actually knew anything about building a PC you would know that you wouldn't need a gig of ram, a 256 mb vid card, or a 500w PSU. If you really need those things, you've got a disposable income and should really think hard about spending it such.

If you're smart, you'll wait until AMD releases socket-939 so that you can have an Athlon FX with dual channel memory without registered DIMMs. You'll also cut out the Radeon XT and purchase a 9800 Pro and overclock the snot out of it. Learn more about computers and building them before you pick some parts and throw them together.
