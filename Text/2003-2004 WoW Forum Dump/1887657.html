 1. My take on the EQ2 character creator | 7/28/2004 5:04:17 AM GMTDT
Well, it finally finished downloading, and i'm going to tell you what I think, like it or not.

The character models themselves without customization have always been fairly impressive to me, and that didn't change when I first ran the program. There are, however, a few complaints I have. Firstly, it lagged. Yes, just this little piece of the game would jerk around everytime I would zoom in/out or move the camera around. I tried to remember that the beta was still in progress and that little things like these would be fixed. Don't try to blame it on my comp either, I did an alt-tab and found that my CPU usage was only at 10%. My second problem with it is that I was able to get only a few of the races to match my look that I wanted for them. Even worse was that none of those races were the ones I plan on playing. The sliding bars are nice and add a lot of detail, but what they need to add is more choices for the hair and other areas where you have to choose a set style. I found that no matter how I tried, I couldn't get the Dark Elven male to not look like a chic. Bald worked of course, but that just looks ugly on an Elf. Finally, while I love the character models, the environmental graphics (what I got to see) weren't all that impressive. Is that left tower in the intro screen SUPPOSED to be square? Even if it is, that thing is hella ugly. Should've just kept it out.

I am in no way saying that the char creation is better or worse than WoW, so don't start arguing over that. When I played WoW, however, I did find the character models more my style even if they were just randomized. If SOE can add a bigger focus on making the characters look actually appealing to players and not so much on being changable, both games will score muucho points in my book. Currently, however, I like WoW's character creation better. That last sentence was opinion, meaning don't be a fanatic about it. I'm not saying WoW is better (although that was a previous account), i'm just saying that right now I like this part of it better because it's more along the lines of what i'm looking for. It may not be as good for you.
