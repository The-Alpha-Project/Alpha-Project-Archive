from another thread http://www.battle.net/forums/wow/thread.aspx?FN=wow-general&T=639501&P=1

Q u o t e:


First, is it true that Blizzard has openly stated there will be no PvP servers (and not retracted it later?). I see alot of folks here speaking as if the decision has already been made, but I cannot find a definative statement by Blizzard that supports the claim they will not have any PvP servers. Can someone link these statements for me? (no heresay please)

Ok, anyhow to begin this discusion, let's all agree on a few things:

1. Many folks have many different opinions.
2. People should not force their opinions (and thus playstyle) on others.
3. There seem to be quite a few folks here who desire to play WoW PvE mostly and many who desire PvP in WoW.

With these facts I am pretty sure everyone agrees upon, I do not understand why there are so many anti-PvP folks desiring there be NO PVP server at all? Why does it bother an anti-PvP person if pro-pvpers have their own server? Seriously, it seems like from many of the posts I have read here that many anti-pvpers actually get ANGRY about any mention of PvP in WoW and I just don't get it.

Surely Blizzard will bow to the "blue" folks who don't want PvP (and the subsequent cash from them), so what is your worry? Why do you care if they put a server or three for those who enjoy player confrontation? Surely there are enough PvPers to support such a server(s).

OK, that aside, let me give my view on the possibilities for PvP in WoW (what I would like to see as a big fan of MMPG PvP).

1. Faction based system where you cannot hit your own faction (unless it is a duel or designated place such as an arena).

2. No level restrictions, because this just leads to enormous possibilitites for exploiting. It just DOES NOT work. I have played under both systems and it SUCKS.

3. No zone restrictions for PvP with the exception of perhaps a "bazaar" type of zone where there might be stuff traded ect (like EQ's Bazaar) Obviosly, there will not be PvP in instances either.

4. To prevent "newby griefing" (which should not be a HUGE problem because it is faction based teams), have strong (but not godlike) guards in newby zones protecting the newbs...or at least giving an advantage to the defending faction over their geograghic areas. In fact, this idea can extend beyond newby zones with thinning or thickening guards based on how close a player is to their home faction's territory. The faction of the guards is NOT exploitable. Opposing factions will/can never get good faction with the guards. Guards super close to the same faction city will begin to see through invis and cloaking ect. Protect the newbs...but do not make it impossible for a strike on newby lands (it will be exciting for a newby to see a big bad enemy uber guys sweeping through their home zone and chill them to the bone!)

I do not know what their plans are now for PvP (as they do not release much info), but I have read some info indicating a selective number of PvP zones (10%) and arenas/duels ect. That's all great and stuff for "blue" folks, but there are MANY folks are there who like randomness in the PvP. The fear that an attacker could be present anywhere you go and the challenge of being that attacker is the excitement we desire and hope for. Not some silly pre-contrived event or /duel.

I am really interested in seeing what folks feel about this. Those that hate PvP will surely make themselves heard (and have), but for those of you who ENJOY player conflicts and wish there to be such a thing in Warcraft, please post your thoughts!
