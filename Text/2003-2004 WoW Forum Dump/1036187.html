207. Re: A Role-Player's Plea [Or Several] | 1/23/2004 7:41:49 AM GMTST
Hopefully the storyline of World of Warcraft, and it's development, will prove as interesting and enthralling as we are hoping.

I've missed an interesting feature of World of Warcraft that we've heard little about, but apparently will be incorporated in the game; Rituals. I'd love to see a large variety of rituals, some for specific classes, others for mixed groups. The main questions on my mind right now are how many rituals will we see, and how will they be obtained or performed? I believe I recall hearing something about a ritual that could be performed by a large circle (20?) of Warlocks, but beyond that, I don't recall any specifics.

Here are a few quick ritual ideas I can come up with off the top of my head. This list will not, by any means, cover every race/class combination. Feel free to contribute other ritual ideas on this thread or tear mine apart. Obviously, these would require a massive amount of balancing; keep in mind these are just rough concept ideas.

Warlock Rituals:
  • Lesser Demonic Summoning. (6 Casters) Summons a Pit Lord, which becomes the pet of the ritual leader. This ritual consumes a large amount of mana and a large ruby with a fairly significant cost.

  • Theft of the Soul. (3+ Casters) This smaller scale ritual is a combined Lifetap spell between several Warlocks. Each Warlock increases the damage the lifetap deals to the target and the amount of hit points restored to each caster.

  • Summon Dreadlord. (9 Casters) This summons a Dreadlord NPC. This NPC can then be attacked or talked to. A significant enough gift would convince the Dreadlord to serve a specific master for a short duration.

  • Summon Eradar Warlock. (18 Casters) This summons one of the most powerful demons of the burning legion. If hailed properly and given appropriate tribute, they will likely cooperate with the Warlocks that summon them. Else, they are just as likely to destroy the mortals that would try to control their power.

  • Bind Demon. (2+ Casters) This ritual temporarily immobilizes a demon. The greater the number of casters, the higher the level of effect (increasing duration and decreasing the chance it will be resisted).

    Priest Rituals:
  • Summon Spirit (3+ Casters): Summons a friendly spirit based on the race of the ritual leader (Spirit of Light, Spirit of the Forest, Spirit of Death, etc). This spirit can become the pet of one of the casters of the ritual.
  • Blessing of the Light (5 Casters; Human or Dwarven leader). Increases holy resistance, attack speed, and generates a fire damage shield for all allies in the area of effect.
  • Feast of Elune. (5 Casters; Night Elven leader) Massive area of effect buff, increasing mana regeneration, movement speed, and improving dark vision.
  • Unholy Ascention. (5 Casters; Undead Leader) All allies in the area of effect gain increased health regeneration, melee resistance, and unholy resistance.
  • Glory of the Darkspear (5 Casters; Troll Leader). Increases the health regeneration, mana regeneration, and cold resistance of all allies in the area of effect.
  • Grand Ressurection (10 Casters; All): Ressurects all players who have fallen in the area of effect.

    Mage Rituals:
  • Ignite the Heavens (3+ Casters): Massive area of effect fire spell. # of casters increases area of effect and damage.
  • Icy Petrification (3+ Casters): Decreases the target's cold resistance, slows their movement rate, and slows their rate of attack. Effects scale to the number of casters.
  • Burst of Lightning. (3+ Casters): Damages and stuns a single target. Stun duration and damage increase with the number of casters.
  • Incendiary Whirlwind (9 Casters): Creates a moving damage effect that deals continuous fire damage to any enemy target within it's range. Can be controlled by the cooperative effort of all 9 casters if they remain still and do not perform any other actions.
  • Force of Eternity (20 Casters): Requires a drop of water from one of the Wells of Eternity. Creates a massive burst of pure mana, dealing massive unresistable damage to a single target.

    Hunter Rituals:
  • Imbue With Nature (5 Casters): Dramatically increases the effective level of a specific Hunter pet, but all other ritual casters cannot use a pet for the duration of the ritual.
  • Find the Way (3 Casters): Powerful tracking ability; creates a path to anything on the same continent. (Players enter target's name)

    Shaman Rituals:
  • Big Bad Voodoo (5 Casters, Troll Leader): All players in the area of effect gain a damage-absorbing barrier that absorbs a few hits before disappearing.
  • Getcha Mojo Working (5 Casters): Increases the Health Regeneration, Mana Regeneration, and Earth Resistance of all allies in the Area of effect.

    Druid Rituals:
  • Peace with Nature (3 Casters): Decreases the agressiveness of all natural creatures in the area of effect. Very large range. (Massive "Pacify" type spell)
  • Mass Shapechange (x casters): Shapechanges all allies in the area of effect into a specific form, up to one ally and the druid for each druidic caster.
  • Cenarius' Horn (20 Casters):Requires a focus item. Summons a large number of Wisps that detonate on the target, massively reducing the mana and spellcasting abilities of the target. In some cases, this will deal hit point damage as well, if the creature is vulnerable to mana tap type abilities.

    Paladin Rituals:
  • Imbue With Holy Light (3+ Casters): Massive "Seal" type effect on a single target, dramatically increasing their effective level for all purposes. The number of casters increases the potency of the buff.
  • Strenth of the Silver Hand (5 Casters): Increases the melee damage, attack speed, and health regeneration of all allies in the area of effect.
  • Weapon of the Light (15+ Casters): Summons a temporary (disappears when the user logs off) Soul Bonded weapon usable by the leader of the ritual. The weapon type is chosen during the casting of the spell. This "Holy Weapon" deals exceptional damage against demons and undead.

    Non-Class Rituals:
  • Banshee's Wail. (5 Casters; Female Undead leader). Deals damage and decreases the resistances of a single target.
