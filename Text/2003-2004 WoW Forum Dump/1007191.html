Here is the proposed option:

It's taken somewhat from DAoC (a game I am familiar with) but has an alternate spin. If there is some way to set up a kind of PvP economy, then a faction vs. faction driven economy can be also similarly established. Like the game, it is natural that this will start slow and at a meager income pace, but if anyone who wishes to engage in PvP has the option of gaining a type of reputation or faction prestige points from killing a player from another faction, then a faction vs faction would give incentive to hunt out other enemy characters on a continual basis.

However, you have to have some penalty to dying as well. Rather than losing items, you lose some of your prestige points, which are obviously being saved up to purchase some item or maybe skill or rank title. Therefore, you artent safe from loss, and you want to take your character to a good level in order to gain the most points with the least amount of loss. If you wish to ever be able to gain some of these items or access to a certain "serve the realm quest" you NEED to PvP but you also must do it well. Opening Quests for those who belong to a guild who pools these Prestige points could govern a chance for certain members to gain a guild centered item or reputation which would create legendary staus. There could be a derived formula which the victor character/team "steals" from the Enemy character/team. Naturally a PvP driven economy opens up a new style of PvP game. It is like leveling in a PvE world because you can gain what we all want when PvPing, a strong more uniquely equiped and well-known character.

A side point I'd like to make is this... You do not HAVE TO PvP if you choose not to. PvP zones as Blizz has mentioned allow you to stay safe from Griefers in instanced dungeons and you can still find unique and awesome items doing it that way as well. Also while i mention griefers... Killing a low level player or exp party out in the PvP zones (there are always some) won't be as beneficial because while you can enjoy a little player /rude you, there could also be a penalty for doing so. You, as a grief troll, could take a shot to your Presitge points or maybe just gain little to no points as well as taking a shot to your faction status with that faction. This will allow for griefing which happens in any game, but has some detering factor (i am thinkig intensely for a better idea)

Anyways, i know that is long, but maybe it is a good point (?) I would like some response, critiques, flames =P, and even additional ideas that i know you have. Maybe it could be implemented or at least help blizz along in one of the most important and rewarding parts of this game.
