I think you guys are missing the point here. Half this thread is discussing the "Graphics Arn't Everything" topic. This is the *wrong* discussion to be having because WoW owns the HELL out of LineageII graphic-wise. You can argue against that point all you want, but you wont understand how I can get off saying this untill you SEE both games.

A while back, I was told by somone who had played WoW that "The screenshots just dont do that game justice". At the time I thought that was BS, but after actually playing both games at E3 I have come to understand what this person meant.

The way the game FEELS while its being rendered is *totally* different than the graphics you see in individual screenshots. The world is IMMENSE and FULL - qualities that LineageII just does NOT match. When you move around, you've got specular pixelshadeing hilighting the textures on the ground and on trees- stuff like that just cant be communicated in screenshots. You can practically FEEL the moonlight and the warmth of the sun! WoW puts you in an inviting and elaborate environment while LineageII is cold, alienating, and simply not as visually stimulating. Sure, you can take some nice looking screenshots from the LineageII engine, but when it comes to PLAYING and engine, WoW is hands-down the champion of mmporg graphics.

You guys will make your judgments and nobody can stop you, but dont make any *decisions* untill you see the game rendering your character in real-time, because I promise you there is a HUGE difference and when people play this game it will hit them like a giant razor-tipped orcish axe.
