 1. Take the November 15 Release Date to the Bank | 10/3/2004 6:14:07 PM GMTDT
We can take this date (or within 7 days of it at least) to the bank. Vivendi Universal, the ultimate corporate parent of Blizzard Entertainment, needs a big fourth quarter. Vivendi's CEO has promised to reduce the company's debt to EUR 5 billion by the end of this year. Further, Vivendi brass does not consider its computer gaming division as a "core" business (in fact, the entire unit - including Blizzard and Sierra - was on the selling block in 2002). Therefore, they aren't worried about the state of the game upon release (not to say that the game will necessarily be in sad shape in a November release).

Further, the gaming division had a bad 2003 - losing $250 million. The division was perceived as taking too long to develop and market games. The head of the gaming division will NOT allow WoW to fall past 2004 and neither will his bosses.

The November 15-22 date range is an important release date because it provides enough cushion to get the game on the shelves prior to the important holiday shopping season that will start after Thanksgiving in the United States. Also, a November release date provides Vivendi with plenty of time to sell the "hoped-for" 2 million copies of the game they hope to move in the United States and Korea prior to December 31, 2004 - the end of its financial year.

WoW is the only product in Vivendi's product pipeline that has the potential to deliver $100-150 million of revenue and significant bottom line impact with incremental additional cash outlay and expense prior to the end of the year. In theory, Sierra's Half-Life 2 could deliver but Vivendi's corporate HQ has no hope of getting that one out the door in November.

What does this mean? I don't think we'll see a simultaneous US/Korean release. We will see a 2004 Korean release date though to get those revenues in the door for the 2004 year. The US release date will be a crunch and the Korean date will lag as a result. Vivendi has superior distribution and publishing capabilities and will have the capacity lined up to get this game out in 2 weeks from a gold date. Here are my predictions:

US open beta begins 10/25
US gold date 11/7
US release date 11/22

Korean gold date 11/22
Korean release date 12/15

European gold date 12/15
European release date 1/5
