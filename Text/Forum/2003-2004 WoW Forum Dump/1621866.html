  • 4) Is player housing still planned? will it be instanced? what about guild housing?

  • 6) Will there be any siege warfare?
  • 7) Regarding NPC's from the horde and alliance that raid eachother:
  • Will they use tactics? Do the NPCs group? Can you group with them?

  • 15) Any chance for a WoW olympics?
