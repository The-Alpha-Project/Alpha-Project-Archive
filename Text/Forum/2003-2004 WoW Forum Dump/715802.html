Illidan Stormrage:

Class: Demon Hunter
Affiliation: N/A

During the dark times, before Ancients War, Illidan was one of the few magic user. Even if his older brother, Furion, warned him about the perverse influence of magic, Illidan continued to improve his magic knowledge. Bit by bit, the Night Elf lost his noble soul because of his dependancy to chaotic energies. After using his powers to help his borther against the demons of the Burning Legion, he finally betrayed its people when he allied with Azshara. After the war, Illidan summoned a new Moon well to make sure his beloved yet corrupting magic would never disappear from earth. Because of this action, Furion ordered that Illidan stay jailed under the surface for eternity.

Illidan was trapped during a very long time, maybe thousands of years. It is only in 624 that Tyrande freed him thinking that Night Elf could help her fight the demons. Wanting to proove his brother that he is still loyal to elves, Illidan killed all Felwood demons.

Manipulated by Arthas, the Demon Hunter used the powers of Gul'dan skull to destroy Tichondrius and his minions. He succeeded in killing the Lord of Nathrezim, but alas, the vicious powers of the skulls turned him into a demon. Later, Malfurion saw him under his new appearance, and Illidan was banned of Ashenvale. He was never seen again.



The ending is incorrect and should be changed to confirm his battle in Outland, service to Kil'Jaeden and the assault and final battle at the Frozen Throne, where he lost to Arthas.

Class: Archmage

Medivhs apprentice, Khadgar became a great hero because of his legendary courage and all he did for Azeroth during the First War. Khadgar was the one that murdered Medivh, destroyed the first portal and died in Draenor, trying to contain destructive energy that could have been fatal to Azeroth.

Also, I suggest writing up descriptions of new characters. Here are the few that I can think of:


Anub'Arak

Class: Crypt Lord
Affiliation: The Scourge

Once one of the leaders of his arachind-like people, the Nerubians, the Spider Lord was a mighty warrior. However, treacherous as he was, he was corrupted and tainted by the Scourge's spreading power.

Anub'Arak was sent by the Lich King as a personal body guard to aid Arthas, and helped the fallen prince make his way to the Frozen Throne.


Kael'Thas

Class: Blood Mage
Affiliation: Illidan / Burning Legion?


Prince Kael'Thas was a proud and noble leader, and after the destruction of the High Elven nation of Quel'Thalas, he led the Elves to safety throughout Lordaeron. He fought alongside the Alliance under the command of General Garithos.

Sentenced to execution along with his brethren for 'treason', he was saved by Lady Vashj and her Naga followers. They escaped to Draenor and swore allegiance to Illidan; they helped him overthrow the Lord of Outland and led the assaults against the Frozen Throne in an attempt to defeat the Lich King. After Illidan's defeat, they went back to Outland and have not been heard from since.


Maiev

Class: Warden
Affiliation: The Sentinels

When Illidan Stormrage had been imprisoned some 10, 000 years ago it was Maiev's duty to keep him caged. When he was let loose by Tyrande, in hopes of aiding the Night Elves in their battle against the Demons, Maiev felt it was her responsobility to hunt down the escapee and encage him once more, if not worse.

She feels a strong sense of discontent towards the Priestess, Tyrande, for letting Illidan free, and almost gets Tyrande killed in Lordaeron. Finally catching Illidan in Outland, she is defeated by the forces of Prince Kael'Thas and Lady Vashj. Maddenned beyond belief by the chase, she has become vengeance itself, forever searching for the Demon. She was last known to be on Outland, following Illidan's tracks, trying to find and imprison him yet again.


Sylvanas Windrunner

One of the greatest defenders of Quel'Thalas during the Scourge's assault, she stood till the last and was slain by Frostmourne at the hands of Arthas. For a long time she was under the influence of the Lich King, but when he had started losing power, her and many of her fallen comrades were given free will and thought. Dubbing themselves the Forsaken, and gaining the allegiance of one of the Nethrazim Dread Lords, she wiped out the other ruling powers and killed General Garithos.

By giving off false information she has succeeded in allying the Forsaken with the Horde, and only now awaits the completion of her own 'plague' which would convert all other living things to her will. She lives in undeath and considers the Lich King a primary target.
