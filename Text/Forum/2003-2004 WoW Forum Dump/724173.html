1. Tauren Druid in Critter Form | 11/7/2003 4:00:11 AM GMTST
I notice that druids of the horde will be able to take on the form of a critter, like a bunny rabbit, to sneak into enemy territory and scout. It sounds like fun, so I was just thinking, if you are in the alliance and you see a bunny acting suspiciously, how can you test if it is a real bunny or a Tauron Druid in Critter Form? I've come up with some possible tests, see what you think.

1. Drop a carrot and some gold on the ground and hide behind a tree. If the bunny hops up and eats the carrot, its a bunny. If it hops up and pockets the gold, its a tauron druid in critter form. If it hops up and pockets the gold and eats the carrot, its a tauron druid in critter form and he is hungry.

2. If you are a warrior, take a large 2 handed war hammer and sneak up behind the bunny. Then gently raise the hammer above your head, and bring it down hard on the bunny. Now gently lift up the hammer. If you see a bunny pancake, it was a bunny. If the bunny is still there but he has an angry (and dizzy) look on his face, its a tauron druid in critter form.

3. If you are a hunter, command your pet (tiger) to pounce on the bunny and eat it. If your pet comes back with a full stomach and a happy look on his face, it was a bunny. If your pet comes back seriously injured with a suprised look on his face, its a tauron druid in critter form.

4. If you are a priestess, go to the bunny with the intention of picking it up, blessing it and giving it a kiss. If everything goes as planned and the bunny hops away happily, it is a bunny. If you find you cant lift the bunny off the ground (because it weighs as much as a tauron) and the bunny starts humping your leg with a crazy look in its eyes, its a tauron druid in critter form.

5. If you are a mage, line up the bunny and cast a level 3 fireball straight at it. When the flames die down check the spot. If there is a small pile of ash there, it was a bunny. If the bunny is still there, but his fur is badly singed and he has a mad look in his eyes, its a tauron druid in critter form.

6. If you are a warlock cast life drain on the bunny rabbit. If you get about 10 hit points out of the bunny, then he falls down dead and the spell cancels, it was a bunny. If after a few mins you find you have taken about 500 hitpoints out of the bunny, and you are still casting, and the bunny is hopping around looking very frustrated, its a tauron druid in critter form.

I cant think of any more tests right now, does anyone else know of a way to check if a suspicious bunny is legit or not?
