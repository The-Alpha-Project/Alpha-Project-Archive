Per the post here: (post has fallen off the board, please see the addendum at the end of this post) there was some good discussion about a faction based PvP server. There was nothing negative about it posted which leads me to desire some numerical data (if you would bear with me). I am just a gamer like you guys, but such a poll (given folks participate) would surely lend aid in their (Bliz devs) decision to either put up a PvP server or not. I am pretty sure they will not or cannot put up such a poll themselves for a variety of reasons. As such, here is the question:

Would you play on a faction based PvP server with no level restrictions and no zone restrictions with guards (un-exploitable) protecting newby zones?

1. Yes
2. No

Please...the questions is would YOU PLAY ON THE SERVER? If not, please vote no. If so, please vote yes :)

The details of such a server are discussed in the above link. What I believe is that this board represents a decent sample of the population that will play WoW (or at least the best one we have easy access to). If 10% of folks say they WILL play on a faction based PvP server, then it seems to justify at least 1 server out of 10 with this rule set. If 100% say they would play on such a server when it goes live....well then...:)

I seriously doubt such a thing, but seriously, if 50% of folks post here that they would play on such a server, the devs would seriously have to consider such a thing (IMHO). As such, PLEASE vote either way just because it would be nice to see how this plays out. THANKS!

Edit note:

The link abive has already fallen off the boards so I am gonna repost the ideas here for a factional PvP server. As you all know Blizzard has claimed the world will have PvP in 10% of it's areas and ONLY on a consensual basis. We believe this will be in the form of some desginated PvP zones like arenas and via duels. Many folks would rather play on a full factional PvP server where the excitement of conflict could happen at anytime. Here are the proposed rules of such a server:

1. No limits on what level can attack any other level.

2. The same rules of grouping and communication as the "blue" servers. No grouping out of faction and little communication.

3. No ability to attack your own team except through duels or designated PvP areas (arenas).

4. No ability to exploit the faction of another team. Example: An Orc will NEVER be able to get good faction with Night Elf guards.

5. Strong (but not godlike) presence of guards in newby zones...thinning out the further one goes from one's home town. The guards should not be godlike in that strikes SHOULD be allowed against opposite faction home areas, but only at a disadvantage with guards protecting and helping the defenders/newbies. Solo players should not be able to just camp newby areas without fear of death, but they might be able to strike into the areas briefly and flee. Whislt newbies should be able to run to guards for protection. This is all about balance, but PvP should happen EVERYWHERE...just HARDER for an attacker on an enemie's homeland. Contested areas will have no guards helping either faction.....can you say...WAR? :)
