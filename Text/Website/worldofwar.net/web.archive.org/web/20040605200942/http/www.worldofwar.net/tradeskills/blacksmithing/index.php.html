What are Blacksmiths?

They forge weapons and armour from metal bars which they can augment with jewels. They can temporarily enhance weapon's also.  They are usually Minors so that they are able to collect their own materials such as copper ore.

How do I become one?

You will need to find a Blacksmith trainer and these are usually located near a forge or anvil. Below is a list of locations. You will be charged skill points to progress through the levels of proficiency.

Blacksmith Trainer Locations

Stormwind : Therum Deepforge, Dwarven District
Ironforge : Bengus Deepforge, The Great Forge {# 1}, Grumnus Deepforge,  The Great Forge {#1}
Undercity : War Quarter {# 5}

Blacksmith Supplier Locations

Stormwind :  Kaita Deepforge, Dwarven District
Ironforge : Thurgrum Deepforge, The Great Forge {# 1}
Undercity : War Quarter {# 5}

After training you will need to buy a blacksmithing hammer at the Blacksmith supplier and if you're a miner too, you'll need a pick to mine for ore.

You train to three levels of proficiency and each has certain prerequisites that need to be met before advancing to the next level.

How do I get Materials?

The most efficient way is to train as a Miner too so that you are able to collect your own ingredients. You can also trade for them from NPC traders or from other players.

What can I make?

Armor and weapons can be created, including items that can not be found at traders or dropped by monsters. Highly prized therefore. Blacksmiths can also create stones which can temporarily improve a weapon's damage. A few items they can make are useful to other classes and Trade Skills. When becoming an Apprentice Blacksmith you are automatically given three recipes, Copper Bracers, Rough Weightstone

You will need to locate an Anvil before you can create items and these can often be located near Trainers/Suppliers {as listed above}.

 To recap basics of Blacksmithing:

  • Training as a Blacksmith costs Skill Points
  • Learning Blacksmith recipes costs money
  • There are Blacksmith specific quests
  • Can create armour and weapons found by no other method in the game
  • It's best to train in the supportive skill Mining so you can collect your own materials for free
  • Trainers/suppliers are usually located near forges and anvils
  • You need a blacksmithing hammer before you can create anything so carry it with you, don't store it in your bank
  • Not all items require you to be near an anvil to create
