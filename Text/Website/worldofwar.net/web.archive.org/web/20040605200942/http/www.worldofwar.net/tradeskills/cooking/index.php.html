What good are Cooks?

Cooks can create dishes to be consumed to heal a character. Food that heals can be purchased from NPC traders but food created by cooks has a greater healing potential so it is also a valuable trading commodity.

 How do I become one?

You seek a Cooking Trainer who for the price of a skill point will train you as an Apprentice cook. You can locate the Trainers and Suppliers in the following area.

Cooking Trainer Locations

Stormwind : Stephen Ryback, Pig & Whistle Tavern, Old Town
Ironforge : Daryl Riknussun, The Bronze Kettle {# 4}

How do I get ingredients to cook?

There are an abundance of food stuffs left as loot from a dead monster. You can also train in the supportive skill of Fishing and provide your own fish for free. Suppliers can also sell food which is handy for the harder to find items.

 How do I cook things?

You will need a recipe for each particular dish you wish to make. Once you've learnt the recipe you have it for life. These can be looted from monsters or bought from a trader or even as quest rewards.

If you have the necessary ingredients you will need a fire to create your dish. These are usually located in towns but if you have learnt the Trade Skill 'Survival Skills' you should be able to make your own fire most anywhere which will save the time of traveling back to the nearest town.

To recap on basics of Cooking:

  • Food stuffs created through cooking have a greater healing potential than those bought from traders or found as loot.
  • Supportive Trades Skills such as Fishing and Survival skills will make Cooking more efficient.
  • Once you have learnt a recipe you know it for life
  • Classes the benefit the most from cooking would include the Warrior and Rogue because they only need to replenish health. Mages would benefit the least because they can conjure food and water with their class skills.
